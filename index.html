Pengyu Zhang


I am a postdoc researcher at Stanford working with Sachin Katti. I received my Ph.D. from UMass Amherst. My advisor was Deepak Ganesan. I received my bachelor and master from Tsinghua University.
I design and implement low-power communication, sensing and computing systems for Internet-of-Things. I invented innovative backscatter communication technologies that provide low-power wireless connectivity, leveraged software-hardware co-design to support dedicated applications, designed runtime systems for intermittently powered computing systems, and improved the security and privacy of IoT devices.
